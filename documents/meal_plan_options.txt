
All dining meal plan options include: All Access 7, 240 Block Plan, 220 Block Plan, 180 Block Plan, 160 Block Plan, 
80 Block Plan, 40 Block Plan, Dining Dollar Plan, and Hello Fresh Plan. Each plan varies in swipes, dining dollars, and pricing.

All Access 7 is a first-year meal plan that includes unlimited swipes, 375 dining dollars, 10 meal exchanges per week, and 5 guest meals. 
The total price is 4,675 dollars.

The 240 Block Plan is a first-year meal plan with 240 swipes, 525 dining dollars, 5 meal exchanges per week, and 3 guest meals. 
The price is 3,880 dollars.

The 220 Block Plan is a first-year meal plan with 220 swipes, 650 dining dollars, and 7 meal exchanges per week. 
The price is 3,880 dollars.

The 180 Block Plan includes 180 swipes or meal exchanges and 900 dining dollars. The price is 3,520 dollars.

The 160 Block Plan includes 160 swipes or meal exchanges and 1,185 dining dollars. The price is 3,520 dollars.

The 80 Block Plan includes 80 swipes or meal exchanges and 650 dining dollars. The price is 1,845 dollars.

The 40 Block Plan includes 40 swipes or meal exchanges and 750 dining dollars. The price is 1,370 dollars.

The Dining Dollar Plan includes 1,050 dining dollars with no swipes. The price is 1,000 dollars.

The Hello Fresh Plan includes 4 meals per week (2x2 meals) for 12 weeks and 150 dining dollars. The price is 775 dollars.
