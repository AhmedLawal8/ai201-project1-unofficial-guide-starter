Buffets at Rathbone have a somewhat wide variety of meals, but the staples like salad bar, burgers/pizza are always there. Non-buffet places are 
good, but personally, the selection has worn somewhat thin after 3 years. And if you're getting a meal plan, do not get the unlimited meal 
swipes unless you're looking to eat at the buffet places 3+ times a day and working out. For more specific meal plans, just search the 
subreddit, lots of good advice on what to get for what you want. 250 and 200 is what first and second years usually do.
