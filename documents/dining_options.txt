Lehigh University dining options include Rathbone Dining Hall, Brodhead Dining Hall, Market X, South Mountain Grill and Deli, Fud Truk, 
The Talon, Kalamata, Hawk’s Nest, Asa’s Place, The Grind at FML, Common Grounds, The Hearth, Purple Pita, Leaf and Ladle, Simple Servings 
at the UC, Hideaway Cafe, Global Cafe, The Pub, Clutch and Go, and additional rotating food truck and retail locations across campus. These 
locations include dining halls, cafes, food courts, food trucks, and retail grab-and-go options offering a mix of buffet meals, sandwiches, 
coffee, international cuisine, desserts, and convenience food.

Market X: Located on the second floor of Building C at Lehigh University's Mountaintop Campus, Market X features modern convenience 
and 24/7 access to Lehigh students, faculty and staff. It offers freshly prepared sandwiches and salads, hand-rolled sushi, 
pre-packaged snacks, microwavable meals, Pepsi products and the freshest cup of Starbucks coffee on campus. Market X, the 
first of its kind in the Lehigh Valley, is completely self-serve and features easy-to-use self-checkout kiosks.Payment Methods for Market X: 
Dining Dollars, Debit, Credit, Apple Pay & Google Pay Market X Open All Day: 12:00 AM - 12:00 AM

South Mountain Grill and Deli: With every ingredient sourced from kosher-certified vendors, South Mountain Grill & Deli features a variety 
of savory classics, all meticulously prepared following Kosher meat dietary guidelines. South Mountain Grill and Deli Hours: Monday–Sunday 
10:30 AM – 8:00 PM. Location: 29 Trembley Dr, Bethlehem, PA 18015.

Brodhead Dining Hall: Tucked inside Brodhead Residence Hall, is a warm, contemporary and inviting place for students to dine. 
Students love this location for its family atmosphere and customized menu. Brodhead Dining Hall Hours: Monday–Thursday 07:00 AM – 09:00 PM; 
Friday 07:00 AM – 02:00 PM. Location: 107 West Morton St, Bethlehem, PA 18015.

Fud Truk: The Füd (actually pronounced "Food") Truk is a mobile culinary unit on Lehigh University Campus. We have been serving seasonal and 
locally sourced food since 2012, and rocking out great sandwiches and food ever since. From locally sourced potatoes for our crispy hand-cut 
fries, to our cage-free eggs for our breakfast sandwiches, we always strive to provide students with a constantly evolving menu made with things 
that are great for you. The menu varies based on the season, but y'all can expect to see a theme of comfort foods. Fud Truk Hours: 
Monday–Thursday 10:00 AM – 03:30 PM; Friday–Sunday Closed. Location: Mobile.

The Talon: The Talon offers an eclectic, seasonal menu that includes signature student-centric favorites. The Talon Hours: Monday–Thursday 10:00 AM – 03:30 PM; 
Friday–Sunday Closed. Location: Mobile.

Kalamata: Experience a delicious food tour of Greece and beyond through authentic flavors of the eastern Mediterranean. Kalamata Hours: 
Monday–Friday 10:30 AM – 08:00 PM. Location: 29 Trembley Dr, Bethlehem, PA 18015.

Rathbone Dining Hall: Bright and colorful with a view that can't be beat! Rathbone features eight culinary platforms that can satisfy any 
craving or dietary need for breakfast, lunch or dinner. We offer an array of options from Simple Servings, our allergy-friendly station, to 
Globowl, which offers international cuisine as well as hand-harvested Halal meats upon request. What truly makes this place special, is the 
gorgeous view overlooking the Lehigh Valley. Rathbone is also a 4-Star Certified Green Restaurant; to learn more, go to dinegreen.com. 
Rathbone Dining Hall Hours: Monday–Thursday 07:00 AM – 09:00 PM; Friday–Saturday 07:00 AM – 08:00 PM; Sunday 09:30 AM – 09:00 PM. Location: 
63 University Drive, Bethlehem, PA 18015.

Hawk's Nest: Hawk's Nest is a food court destination on campus, tucked inside Lamberton Hall and featuring state-of-the-art kiosk ordering. 
Featured concepts include Mein Bowl - This Asian Market offers authentic global flavors and vibrant made-for-me menu combinations from familiar 
favorites to exciting new choices. Hissho Sushi - Enjoy fresh, house-made sushi and build-your-own Poke Bowls. The Nest at Night - Your 
new late night destination featuring late-night favorites such as burgers, quesadillas, milkshakes & more! Hawk's Nest Hours: Monday–Sunday 
10:30 AM – 11:00 PM; Thursday–Saturday 10:30 AM – 12:00 AM. Location: 690 Taylor Street, Bethlehem, PA 18015.

Asa's Place: From classic pancakes to hearty omelets, enjoy delicious morning favorites available all day. Asa's Place Hours: Monday–Friday 
07:00 AM – 07:00 PM; Saturday–Sunday 10:30 AM – 03:00 PM. Location: 29 Trembley Dr, Bethlehem, PA 18015.

The Grind @ FML: One of Lehigh's most popular cafes, The Grind @ FML is located on the main floor of the E.W. Fairchild-Martindale Library 
and offers hot breakfast sandwiches, freshly made sandwiches and salads, grain & noodle salads, fresh baked goods, Peet's Coffee, grab and 
go items and Pepsi products. The Grind @ FML Hours: Monday–Thursday 08:00 AM – 09:00 PM; Friday 08:00 AM – 03:00 PM; Saturday–Sunday Closed. 
Location: 8 E Packer Avenue, Bethlehem, PA 18015.

Common Grounds: Proudly serving Starbucks Coffee, Commons Grounds is the café of choice for that quick caffeine boost. Situated on the second 
floor of Rauch Business Center, Common Grounds features house-made soup, hand-rolled sushi, hot and cold sandwiches, salads and a wide selection
of grab and go items. Common Grounds is also home to our popular Patio Grill Series, which is featured on a weekly basis during warmer months 
of the academic year. Over the years, the Patio Grill has become a huge hit with students, faculty and staff. Please join us this fall on the 
Patio outside of Common Grounds to experience it for yourself! Common Grounds Hours: Monday–Thursday 08:00 AM – 05:00 PM; Friday 08:00 AM – 
03:00 PM; Saturday–Sunday Closed. Location: 621 Taylor Street, Bethlehem, PA 18015

The Hearth: The Hearth offers hand-crafted pizzas, made-to-order pasta, classic Italian sides & more! The Hearth Hours: Monday–Friday 
10:30 AM – 08:00 PM. Location: 29 Trembley Dr, Bethlehem, PA 18015.
